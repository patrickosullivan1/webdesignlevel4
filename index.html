Unwritten Rules of Baseball

The 3 most broken rules and how breaches are punished

Do not swing

The batter should does swing on a 3-0 count.

Breaking up a no hitter

A batter does not bunt to break up a no hitter

Stealing a base

A runner does not steal a base when the team is either behind or ahead by a large margin

The Punishment for breaches of these rules

Grab the icepack

Generally the pitcher will throw the next pitch at the batter

A brawl breaks out

This is a serious breach which will result in a bench clearing brawl

Next batter pays the price

Generally the next batter will pay the price with a pitch thrown at him
